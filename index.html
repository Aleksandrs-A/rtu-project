Gluten-free

This sign marks dishes that do not contain lute. Gluten is a processed wheat product that has a high glycemic index, is rapidly absorbed and converted to glucose. Select this sign if you do not want to see gluten-containing dishes in your personal menu. Do not be afraid that you will not see your favorite pancakes - wheat flour can easily be replaced with rice or corn.

Lactose-free

This mark marks products that are lactose free. Lactose is a carbohydrate found in milk and dairy products. It can cause an allergic reaction, and as a simple carbohydrate, it is easily converted to sugar. Use this sign if you have lactose intolerance, or if you have diabetes.

Vegan-friendley

This mark marks products that do not contain animal products. These products can be safely consumed by vegans, vegetarians, as well as people who are allergic to them for some reason.

Penuts-alarm

This sign means that the product contains peanuts or their products. Peanuts are one of the strongest allergens, so if you are allergic to it, do not choose dishes that are marked with this sign.
